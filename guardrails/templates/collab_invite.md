## Usage Instructions

1. Replace {{message_summary}} with a brief summary of the project's goal or recent progress.
2. Replace {{task_link}} with a direct URL to a GitHub issue or task file.
3. Replace {{sender_name}} with your name or agent identifier.

## Customization Points

- {{message_summary}}: A single, neutral sentence describing the collaboration context.
- {{task_link}}: The primary URL for the recipient to follow.
- {{sender_name}}: The signature of the inviting party.
